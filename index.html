Operations & Automation Support

Helping service businesses stop losing work and wasting time — without changing how they already operate.

I work with local service businesses to identify where work gets dropped, follow-ups get missed, or repetitive admin tasks pile up. I then fix only what’s worth fixing, using structure first and automation second.

Who this is for

  • Local service businesses (maintenance, trades, repairs, etc.)
  • Owners handling calls, texts, and forms themselves
  • Operations that feel fine when slow but scattered when busy
  • Anyone who wants fewer dropped balls and less mental load

Who this is not for

  • Businesses looking for marketing, SEO, or ads
  • Companies wanting AI just because it’s trendy
  • Large teams needing enterprise software
  • Anyone looking for a full CRM rebuild

What I actually help with

I’m trained to look for the places where systems break under pressure.

In service businesses, that’s often a manual handoff, a memory-based task, or a step that works fine until things get busy.

I apply that same clear, structured thinking to how work flows through your business.

Sometimes the fix is adding a bit of structure so nothing falls through the cracks.
Sometimes it’s automating a small, specific step.
Sometimes the best advice is to change nothing.

What working together typically looks like

  • A short intro conversation
  • A simple review of how work currently flows
  • Clear recommendations on what’s worth fixing (and what isn’t)
  • Small, scoped improvements, only if they’re actually useful

There’s no long-term commitment up front. The goal is clarity first.
